About

 

In the year 1966, Vans, formerly known as the Van Doren Rubber Company was born. It all started in the land of dreams, the state of California. Two brothers named Paul and James Van Doren were wanting to create footwear that would be sold directly to California customers without having to wholesale to retail stores.

Although both brothers helped create the famous skate brand, Paul Van Doren is the one who made the company what is is today.
